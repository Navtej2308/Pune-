Historical Places in Pune

Shaniwar Wada

Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification in the city of Pune. Built in 1732, it was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire until 1818.

Aga Khan Palace

The Aga Khan Palace is a majestic building that holds great historical significance. It was built in 1892 by Sultan Muhammed Shah Aga Khan III and is an important landmark in Indian history.
