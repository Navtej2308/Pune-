Pune Tourist Places

Explore the historical forts, serene temples, and beautiful hill stations in and around Pune.

Forts

Fort Name Location Description
Shivneri Fort Junnar Birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, known for its historic significance.
Sinhagad Fort Thoptewadi Famous for the battle fought in 1670 and offers panoramic views of the Sahyadri Mountains.
Rajgad Fort Balekilla Once the capital of the Maratha Empire, it offers trekking opportunities and a glimpse into history.

Temples

Temple Name Location Description
Dagadusheth Halwai Ganapati Temple Budhwar Peth A prominent Ganesh temple known for its rich history and grand celebrations during Ganesh Chaturthi.
Parvati Hill Temple Parvati Hill Historic temple atop Parvati Hill, offering panoramic views of the city.
Pataleshwar Cave Temple Jungli Maharaj Road Rock-cut cave temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, dating back to the 8th century.

Hill Stations

Hill Station Name Location Description
Lonavala Lonavala Known for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, and the famous chikki, it is a popular getaway near Pune.
Khandala Khandala A beautiful hill station near Lonavala, offering serene landscapes and trekking opportunities.
Mahabaleshwar Mahabaleshwar Famous for its strawberry farms, viewpoints, and the ancient Mahabaleshwar Temple.
