Hill stations

Lonavala

Lonavala is a popular hill station known for its scenic beauty and pleasant climate. It is famous for its waterfalls, caves, and forts.

Khandala

Khandala, located near Lonavala, is another popular hill station. It offers picturesque views and is a favorite weekend getaway from Pune.

Matheran

Matheran is a unique hill station as it is one of the few places in India where vehicles are not allowed. It is known for its tranquility and beautiful landscapes.
