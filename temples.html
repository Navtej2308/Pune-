Famous Ganapati Temples in Pune

Shreemant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganapati Temple

The Shreemant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganapati Temple is one of the most famous and revered temples in Pune. It is known for its beautiful idol and grand Ganeshotsav celebrations.

Chintamani Ganapati Temple

Located in Theur, the Chintamani Ganapati Temple is one of the Ashtavinayak temples dedicated to Lord Ganesha. It is known for its serene environment and spiritual significance.

Kasba Ganapati Temple

The Kasba Ganapati Temple is the gramadevata (town deity) of Pune. It is one of the most important temples in the city and holds a special place in the hearts of Pune's residents.
