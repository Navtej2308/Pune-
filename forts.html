Forts in Pune

Shivneri Fort

Shivneri Fort is a 17th-century military fortification located near Junnar. It is the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire.

Rajgad Fort

Rajgad Fort was the capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj for more than 25 years. It offers panoramic views of the surrounding region.

Singhgad Fort

Singhgad Fort, also known as Lion's Fort, is a hill fortress located southwest of Pune. It is famous for the Battle of Sinhagad fought in 1670.
